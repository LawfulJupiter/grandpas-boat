Grandpa's Old Boat

Grandpa got this boat from his father. The two of them used to go fishing together when Grandpa was a small boy. Grandpa loves to take us out fishing in it. Some of our favorite lakes to visit include:

  • Ottertail Lake
  • Hoot Lake
  • Lee Lake
  • Jewett Lake
  • Turtle Lake

At the end of next season, Grandpa is going to donate his boat to the Legacy of the Lakes museum in Alexandria, MN
